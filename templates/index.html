{# ============================================================ Problem statement #}
The problem

The work that creates value is buried under the work that records it.

The work that creates value can't be automated. Judgement calls about direction, the structure of a deal, earning an inventor's trust — these take creativity, non-linear thinking, and human relationships. That's your job.

The work that documents the process can be automated. Researching and reading through documents, writing reports and recommendations, writing up meetings, keeping the record straight. Wilfred does it for you — while you control the input and the output. This way, the work only a person can do no longer waits in line behind the work that AI can do.
